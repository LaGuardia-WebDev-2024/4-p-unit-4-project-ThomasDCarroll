Places to Visit in Arcadia Bay

!! MAJOR SPOILERS AHEAD !!

Blackwell Academy

  • The starting point of the game.
  • Really nice atmosphere (I mean, everyones rich and its a private school so...)
  • The dorms are singles... For High Schoolers...
  • Loved all the school posters and little decorations, stuff like this helped make the world really immersive.


Chloe's House

  • The Madsen household drama was... a plot point...
  • Chloe's room showed so much personality, and I really enjoyed all the little trinkets.
  • I really liked when Chloe and Max strung up the board with all their informaton on the Rachel Amber investigation.
  • Seeing stuff like the scratched out height chart on the wall and all of the drawings they did together as kids really helped show Chloe and Max's bond and I really liked these details.


Two Whales Diner

  • I really liked this area of town, it felt so nostalgic for me and reminded me of when my family would go to the "Dam Diner" when I was younger.
  • I really wish I could try Joyce's cooking. I always got so hungry during these sections.
  • Loved Max and Chloe's debriefs.
  • The diner becoming a safe point during the storm just felt so correct.


American Rust Junkyard

  • Why would anyone EVER make this their main hideout...
  • I liked the idea of this location, it just wasnt for me.
  • Frank's debut scared the life out of me omg.
  • Kind of a nothing burger not gonna lie.
